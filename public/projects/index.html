Projects

My projects in NLP, data science and engineering can be found on GitHub.

My favourite ones are listed below.

async-db-fastapi

  • async-db-fastapi is a repo to experiment with building a FastAPI layer on top of modern NoSQL databases
  • I experiment with conventional keyword-based text stores (Elasticsearch, Meilisearch), graph-based stores (Neo4j) and vector-based stores (Qdrant, Weaviate and LanceDB)
  • The hope is to extend this work in the future with custom GraphQL APIs that allow one to expose existing data in new ways to end users, both within your organization and the outside world.

GenderGapTracker

  • The GenderGapTracker is a Python code base that was used to study gender bias in Canadian mainstream news media.
  • The project was funded by a non-profit, Informed Opinions, whose goals are to amplify the voices of women and female sources in the media.
  • I had the opportunity to work with a huge dataset of over a million news articles, scraped daily from seven Canadian news organizations’ websites, and analyze the data stored on MongoDB via modern NLP tools like spaCy.
  • This work resulted in multiple peer-reviewed publications, which can be found here.
